About Us

Your Trusted Healthcare Partner in Narkatiaganj

Aaradhya Multispeciality Hospital has been serving the people of Narkatiaganj and surrounding regions with superior, affordable multi-specialty healthcare. We believe that every patient deserves hospital-grade care close to home.

Modern ICU & Critical Care

Fully equipped 10-bed ICU with round-the-clock intensivist supervision for critical patients.

24×7 Emergency Services

Immediate trauma care, rapid response team, and dedicated emergency ward available day and night.

In-House Diagnostics

Advanced laboratory, digital X-ray, ultrasound, and ECG for fast and accurate diagnosis.

24-Hour Pharmacy

On-site dispensary stocked with all essential medicines at affordable prices.
